# Logo Design Brief

## Client
**Qualiti Laboratories**

## Tagline
"Provider of Comprehensive Chemical Programs for High Risk Environments"

## Objective
Create a professional, trustworthy logo that represents a company specializing in chemical programs for high-risk environments. The logo should convey:

- **Professionalism** - Trustworthy, reliable, expert
- **Safety** - Emphasize the high-risk nature of their work
- **Quality** - High standards and comprehensive solutions
- **Science** - Technical, laboratory, chemical expertise

## Design Considerations

### Industry Context
- Chemical programs for high-risk environments (industrial, laboratory, emergency response)
- B2B focus, likely serving industries like manufacturing, oil & gas, emergency services
- Needs to appear on documentation, safety materials, uniforms, and digital platforms

### Color Palette Suggestions
- **Blues** - Trust, professionalism, stability
- **Greens** - Safety, environmental consciousness (if applicable)
- **Dark grays/blacks** - Seriousness, industrial
- **Accent colors** - Could use orange/yellow for hazard awareness (carefully)

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for readability
- Possibly incorporate a subtle scientific/industrial feel
- Must be legible at small sizes (business cards, labels) and large (signage)

### Elements to Consider
- Abstract representation of chemical structures or molecules
- Shield or protective iconography (safety)
- Laboratory equipment subtle integration
- Modern, geometric shapes
- Should work in single color for stamping/embossing

## Deliverables

### Primary Logo
- Full logo with wordmark and icon
- Horizontal and vertical variations
- Color, single-color, and reversed (white) versions

### File Formats
- Vector: `.svg`, `.ai`, `.eps`
- Raster: `.png` (transparent background) at various sizes
- Favicon sizes: 16x16, 32x32, 48x48

### Usage Guidelines (to be documented)
- Minimum size requirements
- Clear space around logo
- Color specifications (CMYK, RGB, HEX, Pantone if applicable)
- Do's and don'ts

## Timeline
- Concept sketches: 2-3 days
- Initial designs: 3-5 concepts
- Revisions: 2 rounds
- Final delivery: 7-10 days total
