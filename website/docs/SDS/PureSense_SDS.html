PureSense Sensor Kit SDS Sheet

Product: PureSense Sensor Kit

Description: Modular sensor platform for real-time process monitoring with integrated safety data considerations.

Components: Temperature, humidity, pH, dissolved oxygen sensors, control unit.

Handling: Store at room temperature away from direct sunlight. Use personal protective equipment when handling sensors.

First Aid: In case of exposure, rinse with water and seek medical attention if symptoms persist.

Storage: Keep containers closed when not in use. Follow SDS guidelines for chemicals used with the kit.

Exposure Controls: Use fume hood for volatile chemicals; ensure adequate ventilation.
