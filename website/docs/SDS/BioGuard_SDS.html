BioGuard Kit SDS Sheet

Product: BioGuard Kit

Description: Personal protective equipment and sterile workflow kit designed to enhance lab safety.

Components: PPE pack, sterile consumables, workflow documentation.

Handling: Use PPE as recommended. Follow sterile technique.

First Aid: Rinse skin with soap and water; remove contaminated clothing. Seek medical attention if exposure persists.

Storage: Store in a clean, dry area; keep away from direct heat sources.

Exposure Controls: Use as part of standard lab safety protocols.
